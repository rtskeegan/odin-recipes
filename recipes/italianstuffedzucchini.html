Italian Stuffed Lasagna

Description

This is an easy, quick weeknight meal to use up some leftover zucchini. This recipe combines traditional italian flabors with fresh zucchini to create an unbeatable weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • 2 tbsp Italian-style dried bread crumbs
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2/3 cup frozen burger-style crumbles
  • 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Prep the zucchini: Slice each zucchini in half lengthwise. Hollow out the zucchini. Place the scraped out zucchini into a medium mixing bowl. Combine zucchini, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, burger-style crumbles and pasta sauce. Mix well
  3. Place mixture into hollowed out zucchini. Arrange zucchini in a baking dish, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Bake for 40 minutes.
  4. Optionally serve with fresh-torn basil. Enjoy!
